About Us

Charity Search is an organization dedicated to helping people connect to a nonprofit organization near them. Many people would often want to help out their community but don't know where to begin. Charity Search makes it easy. With just your zipcode, you can begin the impactful journey of community involvement here. Getting connected made easy.
